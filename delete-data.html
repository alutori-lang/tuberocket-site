How to Delete Your Data

TubeRocket respects your right to control your personal data. Here's how you can delete your data:

Option 1: Delete from the App (Recommended)

  1. Open TubeRocket on your device
  2. Tap your profile avatar (top right corner)
  3. Select "Delete Account" from the menu
  4. Confirm the deletion

This will immediately remove all locally stored data including your preferences, settings, and cached information.

Option 2: Uninstall the App

Since TubeRocket stores all data locally on your device, uninstalling the app will remove all stored data including:

  • App preferences and settings
  • Language and category preferences
  • Cached authentication tokens
  • Upload history

Option 3: Email Request

If you need additional assistance with data deletion, contact us directly:

What Data Do We Store?

TubeRocket has a minimal data footprint:

  • On your device only: App preferences, language settings, video category preferences, upload count
  • We do NOT have a backend server — no user data is stored on our servers
  • Authentication tokens are managed by Google Sign-In SDK and stored securely on your device
  • Videos and generated content are never stored externally — they go directly to YouTube

Revoke Google/YouTube Access

To disconnect TubeRocket from your Google account:

  1. Go to Google Account Permissions
  2. Find TubeRocket in the list
  3. Click "Remove Access"

This will revoke TubeRocket's ability to access your YouTube channel and Google account.

Third-Party Data

Some data may have been processed by third-party services during app usage:

  • Google/YouTube: Videos uploaded to YouTube remain on YouTube. Manage them from YouTube Studio
  • Groq AI: Audio transcriptions are processed in real-time and not stored permanently by Groq
  • Firebase Analytics: Anonymous usage data is collected by Firebase. This data is aggregated and cannot identify you personally
