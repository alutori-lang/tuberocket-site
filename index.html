FAQ

Got questions? We've got answers.

Is TubeRocket free? ▼
Yes! You get 3 free uploads every day. No credit card needed. Premium ($4.99/month) unlocks unlimited uploads and batch mode.
How does the AI generate metadata? ▼
TubeRocket uses advanced AI to transcribe and understand your video content. It then generates SEO-optimized titles, descriptions, tags, and hashtags based on what your video is actually about.
Is my YouTube account safe? ▼
Absolutely. TubeRocket uses official Google OAuth for authentication. We never store your password. You can revoke access anytime from your Google account settings.
Can I edit the AI-generated content? ▼
Yes! TubeRocket shows you everything before publishing. You can review and edit the title, description, tags, privacy settings, and thumbnail before uploading.
What languages are supported? ▼
TubeRocket supports 18 languages including English, Italian, Spanish, French, German, Portuguese, Hindi, Arabic, Urdu, Punjabi, Bengali, Japanese, Korean, Chinese, Russian, Turkish, Dutch, and Polish.
Does it work with any YouTube channel? ▼
Yes. Just sign in with the Google account connected to your YouTube channel. TubeRocket works with any YouTube channel, including brand accounts.
